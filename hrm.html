Heart Rate monitor

Introduction

I Am Fit Heart Rate Monitor Chest Strap is a personal monitoring device that allows you to measure/display your heart rate in real time or record your heart rate for a later study. It Is Largely Used To Gather Heart Rate Data While Performing Various Types Of Physical Exercises. This is set to be the standard for the most consistent and accurate heart rate out there through the utilization of advanced materials and optimized construction. Combining the latest in Bluetooth Smart technology with updated algorithms, this smart trap will provide the accurate precision you deserve.

How it Works

Demo Video

The I AM FIT Heart Rate Monitor transmits your heart rate to your phone or tv screen via the ANT or Bluetooth, the data is then synchronized with polar beats app These include data such as calories, intensity, duration, speed average, max-speed, training benefit, fat burn percentage, maps/gps route heart rate zones, distance covered, average heart rate, max heart rate, heart beat per minute are also saved and synchronized
